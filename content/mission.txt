<![endif]-->  </span></span><span style="font-size: x-large;"><b>Mission Statement (Purpose)</b></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size: larger;"><span style="font-family: Arial;">&nbsp;</span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;" class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size: larger;"><span style="font-family: Arial;">&bull; To provide a forum of true Christian education, premised upon our foundation, the Word of God.</span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;" class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size: larger;"><span style="font-family: Arial;">&bull; To honor the Lord Jesus Christ in obedience to the leading and directing of the Holy Spirit.</span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;" class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size: larger;"><span style="font-family: Arial;">&nbsp;</span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;" class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size: larger;"><span style="font-family: Arial;">We recognize that wisdom originates with God, and is not necessarily synonymous with knowledge. God's wisdom, based on the original Hebrew language of the Old Testament, is discernment coupled with understanding, which culminates in intelligence, as read in Proverbs 9:10. Proverbs 9:9 clarifies 9:10 by stating: &quot;Give instruction to a wise man and he will yet be wiser. Teach a just man and he will increase in learning.&quot;</span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;" class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size: larger;"><span style="font-family: Arial;">&nbsp;</span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;" class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size: larger;"><span style="font-family: Arial;">Learning is a life-long process, which is supported and strengthened by study (2 Timothy 2:15). Accordingly, we provide a forum for our students to obtain a quality education in Biblical wisdom by providing practical courses.</span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;" class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size: larger;"><span style="font-family: Arial;">&nbsp;</span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;" class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size: larger;"><span style="font-family: Arial;">In the admonition given by the Apostle Paul to his student, Timothy, there is an urgency to continue in that which he had learned (2 Timothy 3:14). This is the heart of the LBCS curriculum.</span></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size: larger;"><span style="font-family: Arial;">&nbsp;</span></span></p>
<p align="center" class="MsoNormal" style="text-align:center">&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: larger;"><span style="font-family: Arial;">
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><b><span style="font-size: x-large;">Articles of Faith</span></b></p>
</span></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size: larger;"><span style="font-family: Arial;">&nbsp;</span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;" class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size: larger;"><span style="font-family: Arial;">Liberty Bible College &amp; Seminary (LBCS) is a Bible-believing institution which accepts the following declarations as being taught in Scripture. Thus, they are accepted by us as truth and authoritatively reliable.</span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;" class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size: larger;"><span style="font-family: Arial;">&nbsp;</span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;" class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size: larger;"><span style="font-family: Arial;">&bull; The inspiration and authority of Scripture</span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;" class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size: larger;"><span style="font-family: Arial;">&bull; One God, subsisting in three Persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit</span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;" class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size: larger;"><span style="font-family: Arial;">&bull; The creation of the human race in the image of God</span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;" class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size: larger;"><span style="font-family: Arial;">&bull; The fall of man and resultant human depravity</span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;" class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size: larger;"><span style="font-family: Arial;">&bull; The deity of Christ, His incarnation, atoning death, and bodily resurrection</span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;" class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size: larger;"><span style="font-family: Arial;">&bull; Salvation by the grace of God alone through faith in Jesus Christ alone</span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;" class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size: larger;"><span style="font-family: Arial;">&bull; One holy Christian church, the Body of Christ</span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;" class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size: larger;"><span style="font-family: Arial;">&bull; The second coming of Jesus Christ and the consummation of all things</span></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size: larger;"><span style="font-family: Arial;">&nbsp;</span></span></p>
<p align="center" class="MsoNormal" style="text-align:center">&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: larger;"><span style="font-family: Arial;">
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><b><span style="font-size: x-large;">Core Values</span></b></p>
</span></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size: larger;"><span style="font-family: Arial;">&nbsp;</span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;" class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-size: larger;"><span style="font-family: Arial;">We affirm the authority of the Bible and believe in the integration of faith, learning, and ministry. We endorse the practice of distance education as a significant contributor to the educational needs of the religious and global community.</span></span></p>
